Our yerba mate grows in the shade of native Araucaria forest — the same landscape our family has worked for over 50 years. Nothing artificial goes into the soil, and nothing about the process has been rushed to fit a faster harvest cycle.

That patience is also why Triunfo holds one of the more complete certification stacks in the category: food safety, environmental stewardship, social responsibility and regional origin, all verified by independent bodies — not self-declared.

For a sourcing team, that also means something practical: one controlled origin, one traceable chain — not a blend of unverified sources.

A Certification Stack Built for Procurement & QA Teams

Every certification below is third-party verified — documentation available on request.

FSSC 22000

Global food safety certification covering our entire extract production process.

Rainforest Alliance

Sustainable agriculture standard — reduced use of fertilizers and pesticides, pollution prevention, proper waste management.

Fair for Life

Social responsibility certification held since 2006, covering fair labor practices across our supply chain.

Kosher

Held since 2009 — ingredients, facilities and production comply with Kashrut dietary law.

Certified Organic

Grown and processed without pesticides, synthetic fertilizers or chemical inputs — equivalency documentation (NOP or destination-market standard) provided during the quote process.

ESG Seal

Environmental, social and governance certification (Sistema Fiep/Sesi) covering our full supply chain.

IG‑Mathe

Geographic Indication for native shade-grown yerba mate from São João do Triunfo — verified origin and tradition.
